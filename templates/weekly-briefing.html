Launched AI Work Platform with a new seats-plus-credits pricing model and acquired OneAI for voice agent capabilities. Pricing is now harder to predict — credits consumed by AI actions add unpredictable cost. Growing overlap with Act!'s SMB ICP but still no native email marketing.

→ Sales: Anchor on predictable flat pricing and purpose-built CRM vs. a work OS. Email marketing gap remains a strong differentiator.

Launched Pipedrive Pulse in beta — an AI-powered lead sorting and scoring tool using XGBoost to prioritize deals. Currently waitlist-only with no pricing announced. Signals Pipedrive's shift toward AI-first positioning, but the feature is early-stage and still unproven at SMB scale.

→ Sales: Highlight Act!'s shipping AI features vs. Pipedrive's waitlist. Note: email marketing still an add-on in Pipedrive.
Zoho PRODUCT Q1 2026

Q1 CRM release added Workqueue (centralized rep task prioritization), Zia Formula Expression Generator (build formula fields from plain language), and enhanced Smart Prompts with LLM-of-your-choice support. Zoho continues to pile on features — complexity is growing and setup burden for SMBs increases accordingly.

→ Sales: Reinforce Act!'s "up and running in a day" simplicity. Zoho's feature sprawl is a credible pain point in displacement conversations.
